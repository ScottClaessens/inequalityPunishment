In Part 1, all participants will perform the same encoding task.

You will be presented with a set of three letters that form "words" with no specific meaning.

You will also be shown a table with numbers corresponding to each letter of the alphabet.

Your task is to encode the set of three letters by substituting them with numbers from the table.



Example: You are given the word LFA. The Table shows that L=3, F=6, and A=8. So you should enter "3", "6", and "8" in the relevant boxes using your keyboard.

Once you submit a code, the computer will prompt you with another word.

You will have 3 minutes to encode as many words as you can. For each word you correctly encode, you will receive a 30 token bonus payment. There is no penalty for incorrect submissions.

You will get to see how many words you encoded correctly and your bonus payment from Part 1 at the end of the study.
