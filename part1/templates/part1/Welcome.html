Welcome! You are now taking part in a decision-making experiment. You will be paid £2.50 as a show up fee. Depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants, you will be able to earn money in addition to the £2.50 guaranteed by your participation. Please read the following instructions carefully.

It is important that you remain silent and do not look at other people's work. If you have any questions, or need assistance of any kind, please raise your hand and an experimenter will come to you. If you talk, laugh, exclaim out loud, etc., you will be asked to leave and you will not be paid. We expect and appreciate your following of these rules.

During the experiment, you cannot communicate with other participants. If you have a question, raise your hand and your question will be answered privately.

This experiment consists of two parts. The instructions for Part 1 are given on the next screen. You will receive additional instructions for Part 2 after Part 1 is over.
