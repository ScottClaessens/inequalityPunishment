Phase 1

There are 4 decision rounds in Phase 1. In each decision round in Part 2, all group members will simultaneously make an allocation decision.

The task of each group member is to allocate his/her endowment of tokens between his/her private account and the group account. Each token not allocated to the group account will be automatically allocated to members' private accounts.
