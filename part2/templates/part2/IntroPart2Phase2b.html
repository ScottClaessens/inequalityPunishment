Voting procedure

All group members simultaneously vote on whether the fine rate will be 0%, 30%, 60% or 80%. Each group member can vote for one of the possible fine rates. The fine rate that receives at least two votes will apply for the rest of Phase 2. If there is a tie, one of the tied fine rates will be chosen randomly and applied in the rest of Phase 2. Each of the tied fine rates has an equal chance of being chosen.

If none of the fine rates receive at least two votes, then one of the four fine rates will be chosen randomly. Each of the four fine rates has an equal chance of being chosen.

Note that a fine rate of 0% essentially means that no one will be fined for their allocations. Also, a fine rate of 0% means that no one will pay the fixed fine fee (details on the next screen).

After all members of your group have voted, you will be informed of the outcome of the vote in your group, and the fine rate that will apply in your group. The outcome of the vote is binding for your group in all 4 decision rounds in Phase 2.
