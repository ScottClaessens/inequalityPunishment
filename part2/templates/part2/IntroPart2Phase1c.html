Example 1

Suppose you are assigned Type A (80-token endowment) at the beginning of Part 2. One other member of your group is also Type A (also 80-token endowment), and the other two members are Type B (20-token endowments each).

Suppose you allocate 35 tokens of your 80-token endowment to the group account. Further, suppose that the other Type A member allocates 49 tokens to the group account, one Type B member allocates 18 tokens to the group account, and the other Type B member allocates 0 tokens to the group account. In this case, the sum of tokens in the group account is (35+49+18+0) = 102 tokens. All group members receive an income of 0.4*102 = 40.8 tokens from the group account.

  • Your earnings are: (80 - 35) + (0.4 * 102) = 45 + 40.8 = 85.8 tokens
  • The other Type A member's earnings are: (80 - 49) + (0.4 * 102) = 31 + 40.8 = 71.8 tokens
  • One Type B member's earnings are: (20 - 18) + (0.4 * 102) = 2 + 40.8 = 42.8 tokens
  • The other Type B member's earnings are: (20 - 0) + (0.4 * 102) = 20 + 40.8 = 60.8 tokens
