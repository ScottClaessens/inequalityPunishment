In each round, before making allocation decisions, participants will also choose a fine rate.

  • The fine rate applies to any tokens not allocated to the group account.
  • All four members will vote for one of the following fine rates: 0%, 30%, 60%, or 80%.
  • The fine rate that receives the most votes will be implemented for that round, with ties broken randomly.
  • If the implemented fine rate is 0%, there is no fine.
  • If the implemented fine rate is 30%, 60%, or 80%, then each participant in your group will be fined for tokens that they do not allocate to the group account, plus an additional fixed fee of 4 tokens.

Example

The fine rate chosen by majority is 30%.

You keep 10 tokens in your private account. You will be fined 30% of 10 tokens (3 tokens), and will additionally pay the fixed fee of 4 tokens.
