Example

Suppose you have been anonymously placed in a group of four. Every group member is given a token endowment of 50 tokens.

You keep 10 tokens in your private account and allocate 40 tokens to the group account.

The other three group members allocate an additional 60 tokens to the group account, taking the total in the group account to 100 tokens.

This is doubled to 200 tokens and redistributed equally among the four group members.

Each member gets back 50 tokens from the group account.

At the end of the round, you have 60 tokens; the 10 tokens you kept plus the 50 tokens you got back from the group account.
